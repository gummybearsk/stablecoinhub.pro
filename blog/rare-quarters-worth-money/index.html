Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì How do I know if my coin is valuable?
Check the date, mint mark, and condition. Rare dates, low mintages, and errors increase value. Professional grading provides accurate assessment.
‚ùì Should I clean my old coins?
Never clean coins! Cleaning destroys the natural patina and can reduce value by 50-90%. Leave coins in their natural state for maximum value.
‚ùì Where can I sell valuable coins?
Options include local coin dealers, online marketplaces like eBay, auction houses like Heritage Auctions, and coin shows. Get multiple appraisals before selling.
‚ùì Is coin collecting a good investment?
Rare coins can appreciate significantly, but the market is specialized. Focus on quality over quantity and buy from reputable dealers.
‚ùì What's the difference between numismatic and bullion value?
Numismatic value is based on rarity and collector demand. Bullion value is based on metal content. Rare coins often trade far above bullion value.
